Practice Corner

This is a corner established for showing the up-to-date standards for Laslafsorin. As all known, Laslafsorin evolved from English and Spanish while absorbing their grammatical characteristics and chose Chinese characters as its word origins by performing certain transforming regulations. For example, the Chinese character "Jing"(like in word Beijing)means capital. We divided the character into three parts and chose their relative similiar forms in Japanese and Korean and so on. Finally we got iroch(in Latin spelling) and we can know relative words' meaning like Ninroch and Soiroch, the latter meaning Nanjing.
